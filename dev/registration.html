About Author Registration

- A regular paper (including oral presentation and poster paper) requires at least one full non-student registration.
- One full non-student registration can cover at most two papers with 6 pages, i.e. two 6-page papers can be registered under a full non-student registration. A paper allows 2 extra pages, with page fee of 175 USD per extra page.
